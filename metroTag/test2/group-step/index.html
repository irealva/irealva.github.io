Help Tag a Street

For any untagged street on the image, click on the name of the street. Type the name as you see it on the image and place any comments if the tagging is unclear.

You will be paid $0.02 for each correct tag. If as a group, your peer turkers reach 50 tags, then the rate goes up to $0.03. If as a group you reach 100 tags, the rate goes up to $0.04. And 150 tags will get you a rate of $0.05 per tag. The maximum amount of tags allowed is 200.

If you believe the map is completely tagged, please scroll below and check the 'map is complete' checkbox and submit the HIT.

If you wish to do so, you can coordinate a tagging strategy with other workers here: https://tlk.io/crowd2

MetroTag is a citizen science website trying to help the Carnegie Museum of Pittsburgh digitize historical maps. Your work will help historians understand how urban areas have evolved over time.

You will be collaboratively tagging streets with other turkers. As you submit tags, you might notice more tags updated on the map as other turkers work on the same image.
