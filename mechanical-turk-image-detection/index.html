Person-counting Image Task

Your task is simple: to identify how many real people are in a picture.

The most important rule is to count a person if you can see their head. Cases where only partial body parts are present are a little harder. Only count someone if they have a head. On the other hand, if you are unsure whether a figure in a picture is a person, count it as a partial/unsure person.

What to COUNT:
• Count a person if you can see their heads
• The back of a person DOES COUNT as a person (there is a head)
• Children and babies DO COUNT as people

What to AVOID counting:
• Animals DO NOT count as people
• Animations DO NOT count as people
• Reflections DO NOT count as people

How you will get paid

You will be asked to count people in 7 images. The same images will be given to other turkers. If any of the images have more than 50 people in them, we will grant you a $0.80 bonus.


Thanks for you help and please remember to accept the HIT first!
